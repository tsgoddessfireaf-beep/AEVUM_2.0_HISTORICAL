Defining "Difficulty"

The Difficulty rating evaluates the physical state of the digital scan. Easy denotes high-resolution, modern typography or clear 17th-century English. Hard indicates Gothic scripts, significant ink bleed, or complex Latin abbreviations requiring paleographic skills.

Legality & Provenance

This project strictly curates links to institutions like the Bibliothèque nationale de France (Gallica), the British Library, and the Internet Archive's Open Library. No copyrighted translations are included unless explicitly released under Creative Commons.
