## 5. Significator Spotlight Cards
Dedicated visual card per significator: planet glyph, sign, house, dignity

- [ ] Built on the **same brand system as #4** — equal branding weight
- [ ] One card for the querent's ruler, one for the quesited's ruler

## 6. Timing Calendar — *build with a weather eye*
Convert the applying aspect into a real-world window ("Perfection window:
~July 3–9 — watch this week").

How it works, mechanically: the orb between significators (in degrees) becomes
the number; the **time unit** comes from sign quality and angularity —
cardinal/angular runs fast (days), fixed/cadent runs slow (months),
mutable/succedent sits between (weeks). Because the unit choice is judgment,
not arithmetic, the calendar is always presented as a **window, not a date**,
and the practitioner reviews it before it ships to the client. The chart's
moment is sacred; the calendar only translates what the chart already says.

- [ ] Orb → time-unit engine with the cardinal/fixed/mutable × angular/succedent/cadent matrix
- [ ] Practitioner review/override step before the client sees it
- [ ] Calendar card on the timing slide + in the PDF

## 7. Personal Opener Recording
A ~20-second personal voice greeting that plays **before slide 1**.

How it differs from the audio reading: the slide narration (already shipped)
is the *teaching* — your voice walking the client through each slide's
content. The opener is the *welcome* — "Hi [name], this is Dolores, I pulled
your chart for [question]…" — addressed to them by name before the deck even
starts. It's you answering the door before giving the tour.

- [ ] Add an "opener" recording slot to the narration studio (slot −1, before the deck)
- [ ] Auto-plays once when the client opens their shared reading

## 8. Client Portal / Reading Archive — *subscription-tier foundation*
Booked clients log in and see every reading they've ever received.

Yes — this is exactly the spine of a later subscription tier: the archive is
the free hook for returning clients, and tiered access (transit alerts on old
readings, annual review readings, member pricing) layers on top. Parked until
the deliverable items above are shipped.

- [ ] Reading list view keyed to client email/auth
- [ ] Design the tier boundaries before building (what's included vs. premium)
